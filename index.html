When You Go Silent

Pata nahi kab sab change ho gaya…
pehle baatein hoti thi,
ab sirf silence reh gaya.

Tum online hoti ho,
par meri messages ka wait reh jaata hai.

Waiting Hurts

Main reels isliye nahi bhejta
ke tum irritate ho jao,
main bhejta hoon kyunki
tum mujhe yaad aati ho.

Shayad tumhare liye normal ho,
par mere liye tum special ho.

My Sad Side

Jab tum reply nahi karti,
main khud ko samjhata hoon —
sab theek hai.

Par sach yeh hai,
andar se thoda sa toot jaata hoon.

Just One Truth

Mujhe sirf tumhara attention chahiye.
Koi pressure nahi,
koi expectation nahi.

Main bas yeh chahta hoon
ke tum mujhse baat karo,
thodi si bhi chalegi.

You’ll Always Matter

Mujhe tum pasand ho.

Isliye main baar-baar text karta hoon.
Isliye wait karta hoon.

Main perfect nahi hoon,
par mera care real hai.

Chahe tum jo bhi decide karo,
tum mere liye hamesha special rahogi.

— from someone who cared deeply
